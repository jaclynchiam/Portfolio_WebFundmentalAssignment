About me

Hi! I’m a first-year Computer Science student at Sunway University with hands-on experience in Java, Python, JavaScript, SQL, HTML, and CSS.

I am passionate about software development, I enjoy coding and solving complex problems using my thoughts, and I am eager to build something useful that benefits people’s daily life. My goal is to become a software engineer after graduation and contribute to creating innovative software solutions.

Background

I’m a former table tennis state player from Penang. When I was 19, I came across a random YouTube video called “A Day in the Life of a Software Engineer at Twitter.” That video really opened up my mind, the job looked so cool. They were solving problems, thinking logically, and building real things with code. That’s when I started getting curious about software engineering.

At that time, I was still studying for my diploma in Business Studies from another college. But slowly, I found myself more interested in tech and coding. After finishing my diploma at the age of 20, I decided to switch paths. Instead of pursuing a business degree, I came to KL and enrolled in a foundation programme at Sunway College, and then continued with a degree in Computer Science at Sunway University to chase my passion in this field.

I'm especially inspired by how software can improve people’s lives, whether through healthcare systems, smart devices, or user-friendly apps.
